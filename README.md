# About the project

The hardest part of saving is not starting to set money aside, but not spending what you've saved. Usually, saving money is hard precisely because it stays right in your pocket. And the larger the amount becomes, the easier it is to find an "urgent" reason to spend a little. Over time, holding onto savings only gets harder: the accumulated capital relaxes you and gives a false sense that there is already plenty of money and taking a bit won't hurt.

Our platform flips this mechanic. The project is based on the idea of immutable financial commitments. You set the rules of the game, and the blockchain ensures they are followed.

You only need to show resolve and make the hard decision once — when creating the contract. You fix your goal and a comfortable deposit schedule, and the smart contract takes on the role of an impartial controller, securely locking the funds away from your own impulsive spending.

An amazing paradox emerges: the further you progress, the easier it becomes to continue. Breaking the schedule will result in the loss of already accumulated funds. The blockchain turns the fear of loss into your main incentive to reach the end.

## Contract as an NFT Safe

Your commitment is deployed on the TON network as a separate NFT. The token acts as a programmable financial vault that obeys no one but its predefined algorithm:

- **Goal creation:** You choose an asset (native TON or any Jettons), specify the target amount you want to accumulate, the regular deposit size, and a time interval (e.g., 7 days).
- **Deposit window:** The interval is not a strict schedule, but a comfortable period of time given to you to make the next payment. For example, if the interval is a week, you have exactly 7 days to make a deposit. You cannot deposit money before the new period starts or deposit an amount larger/smaller than agreed. This protects the process from chaotic actions.
- **Win status (Success):** As soon as the sum of all your regular deposits reaches the target mark, the contract is considered successfully completed. The safe opens, and you can withdraw all accumulated funds to your wallet in one click.
- **Lose status (Defeat):** If the interval allocated for the deposit expires and the deposit has not been received, the contract is permanently locked. You lose your status as an active saver and lose access to the funds already deposited. No exceptions and no second chances.

## Hidden piggy bank (Additional assets)

Your NFT contract acts like a real safe and can receive any other tokens and TON via direct transfers, in addition to regular deposits. These funds are securely locked inside the NFT until the contract is successfully completed. This is a great way to protect spare money from impulsive spending and create an extra incentive to reach the end — because if you violate the contract conditions (Lose), these additional assets will be lost along with the main deposit.

## Transfer Types

Your smart contract is a real NFT asset that appears in your TON wallet alongside other tokens immediately after creation. This means you can manage your financial commitment as easily as any other digital item.

To cover different life scenarios, the platform features a system of flexible ownership rights. You choose the initial rights level when creating the contract, but you can change it at any time later. However, there is an important rule: the rights level can only be "downgraded" (made stricter), never the other way around. This ensures that the discipline framework you set cannot be bypassed:

### 🔄 Transferable
The contract can be freely transferred to other wallets or sold. Suitable for flexible financial strategies.

### 🎁 Gift
The contract can be transferred to another person only once. After gifting, it is permanently bound to the new owner.

### 🔒 Soulbound
The contract is permanently bound to the current wallet. It cannot be sold or gifted. Maximum strictness mode.

## Marketplace

Financial circumstances can change, and sometimes a user needs money before the contract is completed. Owners of Transferable contracts can list their active commitment for sale. The buyer acquires the NFT along with all locked assets inside and takes on the obligation to complete the contract. This creates a unique secondary liquidity market for locked assets.

## Transparency & Security

### 📖 Open Source
The logic of smart contracts and the client side is completely open.

### ⛓️ Decentralization
The platform has no access to your funds. All assets are stored exclusively on the balances of your personal NFT contracts and are managed by the math of the TON blockchain.
